Welcome to the TM Forum Digital Maturity Assessment

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this important survey measuring the digital maturity of your organization.

Today we begin to measure your impressions of present-day capabilities in your organization and a target state that you believe the organization should be working towards in the two-year or other timeframe as set by your assessment leader.

This assessment is a series of statements intended to help you and your colleagues think about and visualize, in very specific and measurable terms, your next digital transformation effort. We encourage you to be as conscientious as possible while completing the survey.

You may save your survey in progress, at each screen. Be aware that responses you provide are shared, in anonymized form, for the purpose of aggregation and benchmarking. Respondent level data is encrypted and safeguarded against security breaches. Please click 'Take Survey' here or at the top of the screen to begin.
