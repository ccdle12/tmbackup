Leader - The 'Leader' manages the whole process from 'Gather The Team' through to 'Publish Results'. This person has the ability to also change the overall results through the Adjust Aggregates screen.
Consultant - The 'Team Leader' can delegate responsibility to a 'Consultant' who can do everything the Team Leader can do.
Evidencer - An 'Evidencer' gathers organizational evidence about progress in your organization's transformation project and can be entered it into the App before the wider team takes part. Evidencers can only see their own results from the survey.
Team - The 'Team' are the people who take the survey. They can see their own results but not other team members results. They may be invited to attend a wider organizational stakeholder workshop to discuss the entire organization's survey results..
